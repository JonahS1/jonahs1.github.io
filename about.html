About Us

The High Technology High School Performing Arts Club (HTHS PAC) is one of the largest and most popular clubs at High Tech. A student-run club, PAC puts on two shows each year, the fall play and the spring musical. Each of these shows has tons of opportunities for students to get involved.

Audition information is emailed out to students on the mailing list and is posted under the Auditions section of the site. Anyone interested in acting, singing, or dancing in the productions is encouraged to try out. Even if you don’t have any experience, come try out! PAC is the perfect place to try your hand at performing arts, whether it’s in a show or at improv meetings.

In addition, PAC forms a band every year that plays during the intermission of the fall show. For the musical, the band plays the songs to accompany the actors, singers, and dancers. No audition is needed to join the band, and all instruments are welcome!

Not interested in performing? There are still many ways to get involved. The stage crew, for instance, builds all the sets and props for all the productions, a fantastic opportunity for those who have a passion for visual art. They also work behind the scenes to help the show move smoothly by facilitating set changes and keeping track of props. The tech crew runs the sound and lighting for all the shows. We also recruit people to help choose and create costumes, as well as to do hair and makeup for the performers.

Whatever your passion, you’re sure to find your place at PAC. PAC is a fantastic way to make new friends and unforgettably fun memories.

Questions? Feel free to reach out to our advisor or any of our officers!
